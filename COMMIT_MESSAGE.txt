## Why USYC Over Uniswap?

- ✅ Single chain (Arc) vs cross-chain complexity
- ✅ TradFi yield (US treasuries) vs DeFi risk
- ✅ Instant redemption vs pool dependency
- ✅ Circle-audited contracts vs custom integration
- ✅ Lower gas costs (no bridging)
- ✅ Simpler architecture (~315 lines vs ~500+)

## Key Features

1. **Automated Yield**: Threshold-based policy execution
2. **Manual Claiming**: Users control when to claim yield
3. **Position Tracking**: Principal and yield tracked separately
4. **Security**: Optional Address Book allowlisting
5. **Instant Redemption**: No lock-up periods

## Architecture

User deposits USDC → Policy executes when balance > threshold →
Excess USDC converted to USYC → Earns treasury yield →
User can claim yield or withdraw position anytime
